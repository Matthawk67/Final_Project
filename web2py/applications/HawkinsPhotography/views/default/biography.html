Matthew Hawkins

  My name is Matthew Hawkins and I am a photographer from Santa Cruz, California. My photography career first began as a Freshman at the University of California, Santa Cruz in which I studied photography and photographic arts. These first few classes became a catalyst for what would become a major passion and eventually small business for me.

  Over the past four years, I have been photographing everything from people to landscapes to the night sky. I have done everything from head-shots, couples photos, family photos, graduation portraits, event photos including concerts, performances and recitals. I have a large portfolio which covers a wide variety of disciplines in photography



I absolutely love what I do and I hope my work displayed here shows that.
